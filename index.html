Our History

Started in 2016, Megathon is an ambitious hackathon organised jointly by E-Cell IIIT Hyderabad and E-Cell IIT Hyderabad with the intention of promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship and development among the student community in and around Hyderabad. The first 2 editions of Megathon turned out to be huge successes with participation from 80 teams having around 450 participants and over 1000 aspirants from 85+ colleges in the first year which rose to over 600 participants and 1000 aspirants.
